Who We Are

A Bridge Back to Stability

Valor Rising LLC is a professional Veteran transitional housing operator based in Jacksonville, Florida. We provide safe, structured, and dignified short-term housing for Veterans experiencing homelessness — serving as a reliable bridge to permanent, independent living.

We are a mission-driven organization committed to honoring the service of every Veteran who walks through our doors. Every decision we make is guided by dignity, accountability, and hope.

Founded by a registered nurse with over 20 years of clinical experience serving Jacksonville communities across multiple sectors of healthcare — Valor Rising brings professional care and compassionate leadership to every aspect of operations.

"We believe homelessness is not a destination — it is a detour. Every Veteran who comes through Valor Rising carries within them the strength, discipline, and courage that no circumstance can erase. Our role is to create the conditions where that strength can rise again."

— Valor Rising, Core Mission

Our Program

What We Offer

🏠
Safe, Furnished Housing

Clean, fully furnished rooms in stable Jacksonville neighborhoods. Move-in ready and maintained to HUD Housing Quality Standards.

📋
HUD-VASH Vouchers Accepted

We work directly with the Jacksonville Housing Authority and VA HUD-VASH coordinators for seamless placement and payment.

🎯
Structured Program Environment

Clear house rules, quiet hours, substance-free environment, and a weekly check-in process designed to support stability and forward progress.

🗺️
Housing Stability Planning

Every resident works toward a personal Housing Stability Plan — covering housing, employment, benefits, health, and life skills goals.

🤝
VA Resource Coordination

We connect residents to VA services, community programs, employment resources, and supportive services — working alongside their VA case managers.

💪
Professional House Management

Compassionate, professional house management available to residents. We treat every Veteran with the dignity and respect their service deserves.

Veterans

Who We Serve

  • Veterans experiencing homelessness in the Jacksonville area
  • Veterans transitioning from VA medical care or treatment programs
  • Veterans referred by VA HUD-VASH coordinators and social workers
  • Veterans referred by VSOs — VFW, American Legion, DAV, and others
  • Veterans who are ready to stabilize their lives and move forward

To refer a Veteran or discuss placement availability, contact us directly. We respond promptly and treat every referral with urgency and care.
